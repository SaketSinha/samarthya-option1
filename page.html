Samarthya

Democratizing Governance of Government Schools

Our Just Cause

Empower people to have voice and agency to represent their interests and participate in the governance of their local public institutions.

Our Intender Outcomes

Long term outcome (8-10 years):

Culture of shared ownership around public schools

Medium term outcome (6-7 years):

Foster highly functional quasi legal inclusive governance structures (School Management Committees) for public schools

Medium term outcome (4-5 years):

The local stakeholders have the voice and the agency to actively take part in the governance and oversight of their neighborhood government school.

Short Term Outcome (3-4 years):

Samarthya will create local human resources who would have the capacity to support local people on solving local issues faced in the public school and capacitate them on governing and overseeing public schools .
