Democratizing Governance of Government Schools

Our Just Cause: Empower people to have voice and agency to represent their interests and participate in the governance of their local public institutions.


Bharti

Bharti ji is a SMC member of a school in Saboli area of North East Delhi. She visits the schools thrice every week to ensure everything is in place. After her daughter completed 10th Grade and left that school, it was her ‘concern’ if she would be able to still serve the school. It has been almost an year, since her daughter moved to a new school, but she still continues to assist the new SMC members, work with school officials and local community members to get the work done in school.
