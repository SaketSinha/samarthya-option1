Aamna Begum

Amnaji has been overlooking midday meal distribution in a school in Mohan Garden for the past 6 years. People say there has hardly been a day when Aamna Begum has not been present to ensure proper mid day meal distribution. She attended the Samarthya training session in 2016 where she got to know of the proper norms to be followed for mid day meal distribution. She still continues her midday meal distribution inspections, now equipped with proper knowledge about the norms and standards. She is a true parent leader.
