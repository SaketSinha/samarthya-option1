Democratizing Governance of Government Schools

Our Just Cause: Empower people to have voice and agency to represent their interests and participate in the governance of their local public institutions.



Our Work





Our Achievements

  • Across 27 schools, SMCs have been able to resolve 60% of the grievances raised in SMC meetings - an unheard of grievance redressal rate in public schools.
  • An average increase of 50% in attendance in Parent Teacher Meetings
  • Out of the SMCs they work with, the percentage of SMCs which met more than 12 times in a year went from 27% to 80%
  • Now 83% members are aware of the needs of the school leading to informed and inclusive decision making at school level.
  • Reached 86 more schools in Delhi and Haryana by forging partnerships.
  • Samarthya was recognized as an impactful idea at the Samvidha Sandbox.
